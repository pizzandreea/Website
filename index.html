About us

Our Motivation

Here, we strive to have a personal relationship with every costumer. We invest time to advise you on proper bird care, training, breeding and nutrition - anything there is t to know. Our relationship with you is a personal one and we take your concerns seriously. We’re dedicated to creating a relationship with you that will keep you coming back time and time again. For more details, you can find us at our contact page. Stay informed!

Our promise

We ensure you that all pets are selected with outstanding pet criteria in mind. We review the overall health of each animal using our combined years of experience in the field, looking at such factors as age, size, coat, behaviour, unique markings, overall appearance and desirability as a pet.
